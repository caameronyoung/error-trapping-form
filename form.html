About Our Qualification in Website Design

This course will teach you a variety of programming languages commonly used in industry to create websites. For example, there will be courses on: HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, PHP, & SQL.

Similarly, there will be courses exploring and tutoring you on common stylistic choices for graphical design of pages. This will allow you to have a strong understanding of popular and trending design methodologies to create sites that will be popular and easily accessible

Alongside this, you will also learn about effective communication with clients and co-workers to explain and discuss ideas and visiosn you may have in mind.

To top it all off, it will also leave you with a varying portfolio showcasing the skills you've gained in various programming languages, along with websites you've created.
